## Day 1 — German Basics (A1)

### Alphabet & Sounds (important)

German is **phonetic**.

* **A** = ah
* **E** = eh
* **I** = ee
* **O** = oh
* **U** = oo

Special letters:

* **ä / ö / ü** → similar to “e / o / u” but with lips rounded
* **ß** = ss
